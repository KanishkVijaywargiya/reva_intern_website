REVA University Technology Business Incubator (TBI) aims to build the entrepreneurial eco system by empowering and nurturing student start-ups and incubating innovative technology based start-ups at REVA University campus.

Incubation

  • VISION
  • MISSION

To create an ecosystem that will foster and support entrepreneurship by translating innovative technology ideas into successful ventures offering products, processes and services that greatly benefit the society at large.

To provide conducive support system that helps in nurturing technology based innovative start-ups towards sustainability and scalability.

Objectives:-

  1. 1. To sow the seeds of innovation and creative thinking among the youth of REVA University in particular & society in general

  2. 2. To make students realize value of having great ‘entrepreneurial’ & ‘enjoyable journey’ mind set, irrespective of their career goals in life

  3. 3. To motivate students to develop business ideas on their own and mentor them to turn them into real business ventures.

  4. 4. To establish business ventures, run by the students and powered by REVA Technology Business Incubator.
