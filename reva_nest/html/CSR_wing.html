SMART Centre Inauguration by TMF- REVA University

REVA University joined hands with Tech Mahindra Foundation CSR Initiative to empower youth of the country by Inaugurating SMART Centre at REVA University Campus.

Chancellor Dr.P.Shyamaraju and Vice Chancellor of REVA University Dr.S.Y.Kulkarni said through corporate social responsibility REVA University and TMF commits   to empower youth to build strong nation. Chief Guest of the event highlighted the objectives and Vision of SMART Centre TMF across PAN India. Mr.Shamsher puri Managing Director DTSS, shared statistics on requirement of skilled resources in Infrastructure Maintenance Space.

Shettigere Village to develop as SMART Village:-

As per the initiative and guidelines of Government of Karnataka, REVA University adopted Shettigere Village, Jala hobli near International airport, to develop it as a SMART Village.

CSR FOR WHIRPOOL

REVA UIIC, in association with Team Lease executed the CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) project of Whirlpool in the month of June 2017. For the first batch, there were 25 students who got the benefit out of this project. Certified students entered into OJT (on job training) with Rs. 7250/- as Stipend.

Walkathon

As part of community outreach program UIIC, REVA University joined hands with Motherhood India for the Nobel task “ Beti Bachao Beti Padhao walkathon" on Daughter's day (24 Sep 2017). The registration fees of this event will go to Swanthana charitable trust which houses 50 girls between the ages 8 months and 15 years, who are mentally challenged and have multiple disabilities.
