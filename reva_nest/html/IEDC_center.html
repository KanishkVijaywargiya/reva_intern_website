Innovation & entrepreneurship Centre (IEDC)

  1. Home
  2. Gallery

Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) is functioning in the University with the intention of nurturing Innovation & entrepreneurship skills among the students. It provides a platform for the students to pursue entrepreneurial activities and also provide assistance to potential entrepreneurs.

IEDC Mission

  • VISION
  • MISSION

To identify, encourage and nurture NextGen entrepreneurs with innovative thinking in realizing their ‘Out-of the box ideas’ to resolve problems of the society.

To realize the vision of Innovation and Entrepreneurship Center through networking student enterprises from campus to seed funds and angel investors to transform new ideas into action-driven start-ups.

To act as a catalyst for promoting collaboration amongst academicians, scientists and industrialists to achieve competitiveness in developing the technical manpower for socio economic development under the present scenario of global competition and challenges.

Objectives:-

  1. 1. To sow the seeds of innovation and creative thinking among the youth of REVA University in particular & society in general

  2. 2. To make students realize value of having great ‘entrepreneurial’ & ‘enjoyable journey’ mind set, irrespective of their career goals in life

  3. 3. To motivate students to develop business ideas on their own and mentor them to turn them into real business ventures.

  4. 4. To establish business ventures, run by the students and powered by REVA Technology Business Incubator.

Start-up Investor Meet 2018:-

REVA University Industry Interaction centre conducted Start-up-Investor meet 2018 event on 1st March 2018 on behalf of REVA Research conclave event. In this Start up Investor meet several Product displayed and several companies are participated like, Dexterous, Qsock, GUHAM TECH, Axis Allianz, Embright InfoTech, CAD Centre, Festo Exotainer and Techno Quantum.

Entrepreneurs’ Day:-

Entrepreneurs are those who look for an opportunity and makes the best out of it, taking calculated risks with proper planning and determination. To create entrepreneurial spirit among students, we called it as Entrepreneurs’ Day, where several students and faculty members came out with business ideas and demonstrated and sold their products within the campus through their respective stalls. There were 12 – 15 variety of products being displayed and sold.

Entrepreneurship Week – 2018:-

The Centre for Entrepreneurship Development organized “E-Week 2018”, an event aimed at creating a platform for all the young minds to come up with their innovative business and social development ideas.

Sales force Trailblazer Business Administration specialist:-

Sales force Trailblazer Business Administration specialist training (Online) participation on 6-8 Nov 2017. 3 days’ workshop for students in REVA University on "Sales force Fundamentals for Business Administration Specialists". The Sales force Fundamentals for Business Administration Specialists course provided an understanding of what’s possible with the Sales force platform, its specific applications, and their business values and benefits.

Faculty Development Program on “Roles & Responsibilities":-

University Industry Interaction Centre organized one day Faculty Development Program on “Roles & Responsibilities of Faculty members in Entrepreneurship Development”.
